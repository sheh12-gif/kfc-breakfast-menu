What Makes KFC Breakfast Worth Trying?

KFC is famous for its fried chicken, but the morning menu deserves just as much attention. The KFC breakfast menu brings that same bold, Southern-inspired flavor to the first meal of your day. From biscuit sandwiches loaded with crispy chicken to hearty bowls and fresh sides, there is something for everyone.

Whether you are a regular visitor or trying KFC breakfast for the first time, this guide covers everything you need to know, including items, prices, hours, and a few tips to get the best value.

KFC Breakfast Hours: When Does Breakfast Start and End?

Knowing the exact KFC breakfast hours helps you plan your visit so you don't miss out.

Weekday Hours

  • Start Time: 6:00 AM
  • End Time: 10:30 AM (Monday through Friday)

Weekend Hours

Most locations also start at 6:00 AM and end between 10:30 AM and 11:00 AM on Saturdays and Sundays.

Not every KFC location offers breakfast. It is a good idea to call your local restaurant or check the KFC app before heading out. Drive-thru breakfast tends to be available at the same hours as dine-in.

Most Popular KFC Breakfast Items You Should Try

If you are not sure what to order, these are the top picks customers love the most.

Chicken Biscuit

A warm, flaky buttermilk biscuit with KFC's signature crispy chicken fillet. Simple, satisfying, and packed with flavor. It is the best seller on the breakfast menu for a reason.

Bacon, Egg & Cheese Biscuit

This classic combo hits every note. Smoky bacon, fluffy egg, melted cheese, all inside a fresh biscuit. It is filling without being heavy.

Famous Bowl (Breakfast Version)

KFC takes its famous layered bowl concept and gives it a morning twist. Expect scrambled eggs, hash browns, cheese, sausage gravy, and crispy bites all in one bowl. It is perfect if you want something hearty.

Cinnabon Biscuit

For anyone with a sweet tooth, the Cinnabon Biscuit is a warm, cinnamon-glazed treat. It pairs perfectly with a cup of KFC's freshly brewed coffee.

Tips to Save Money on KFC Breakfast

Getting a great breakfast at KFC does not have to be expensive. Here are some smart ways to save.

  • Use the KFC App: Look for exclusive breakfast coupons and combo deals that are only available through the app.
  • Order Combo Meals: Individual items cost more. Combos bundle a sandwich, side, and drink at a lower total price.
  • Check for KFC Tuesday specials: KFC often runs weekly promotions. The popular KFC Tuesday specials sometimes extend to breakfast items at select locations, so keep an eye out.
  • Go for the Sausage Biscuit: At $1.99, it is the most affordable and filling option on the breakfast menu.
  • Skip Premium Drinks: A regular coffee saves you a dollar or more compared to juices and specialty drinks.

Does KFC Serve Breakfast All Day?

No. KFC breakfast is only available during morning hours, typically ending at 10:30 AM. After that, the restaurant switches to its regular menu featuring items like the KFC chicken bucket and other lunch favorites. There is no all-day breakfast option at KFC as of 2026.

Is KFC Breakfast Available for Delivery?

Yes, many KFC locations offer breakfast delivery through platforms like DoorDash, Uber Eats, and Grubhub. You can also order through the KFC app or website. Just make sure you place your order within breakfast hours, as the menu switches over to lunch at 10:30 AM sharp.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time does KFC start serving breakfast?

Most KFC locations start serving KFC breakfast at 6:00 AM. Some locations may open slightly earlier or later, so checking with your local restaurant is always a good idea.

What are KFC breakfast hours on weekdays?

KFC breakfast hours are generally from 6:00 AM to 10:30 AM, Monday through Friday. After 10:30, the regular lunch menu takes over.

How much does KFC breakfast cost?

The KFC breakfast menu with prices ranges from about $1.29 for a single biscuit to $5.49 for a full platter. Combo meals offer the best value overall.

What is the KFC weekend breakfast timing?

The KFC weekend breakfast timing is similar to weekdays at most locations. Breakfast is served from 6:00 AM to 10:30 AM on Saturdays, and some locations extend service until 11:00 AM on Sundays. Always confirm with your local KFC.

Does every KFC location serve breakfast?

No. Not all KFC restaurants have a breakfast menu. Availability depends on the location. Use the KFC app or call ahead to confirm before visiting.

Final Thoughts

The KFC breakfast menu offers a solid lineup of affordable, flavorful morning options that stand out from the typical fast food breakfast scene. The buttermilk biscuits alone are worth the visit, and when you pair them with crispy chicken or sausage, you get a breakfast that is hard to beat.

Plan your visit within KFC breakfast hours, check for deals on the app, and do not sleep on items like the Famous Bowl or the Cinnabon Biscuit. Whether you are grabbing a quick bite on your commute or feeding the whole family on a weekend morning, KFC has you covered.
